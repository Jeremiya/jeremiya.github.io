I did my BSc degree in Computer Science in 2008 from Hawassa University, Ethiopia. From September 2008 to 2010, I worked as a graduate assistant at University of Gondar . I have been working at Bahir Dar University, Institute of Technology, as an assistant lecturer from September 2010 to 2011. I studied my MSc degree at University of Trento, Italy, focusing on data management and internet technologies - advised by Prof. Themis  Palpanas. Before joining Aalto University, I was an Intern student from August 2012 to Nov 2012, in the position of Junior Researcher, at University of Illinois Research Center (Advanced Science Research Centre (ADSC)), in Singapore.
